About Me

  • I received my Bachelor's Degree in School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering (EE) of Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), China at June 2016.

  • I am pursuing my Master's Degree at SJTU too and will get the degree at March 2019.

  • I'm a geek🐶 curious on programming. I feel uncomfortable when I saw others writing ugly codes. I like to write the high performance and elegant codes.

  • I am focusing on Deep Learning these two years. My research interests include computer vision, lip content recognition and authentication. I follow closely the recent state-of-the-art object detection methods. However, I'm quite knowledgable about sequence learning and have my own awesome ideas on lip-reading task (a paper is in progress for CVPR2019).

  • What about the engineering skills, I am skilled in c/c++, python, java. Beyond these, I can quickly transfer to a new language or new area if needed (I think they are almost the same except for the rules). I have written several projects in several language actually:

    • A c library jpeg_toolbox_python with both c API and python API. The library is to extract the DCT coefficient of the JPEG images.
    • My implementation of paper LipNet: End-to-End Sentence-level Lipreading using Keras(Tensorflow backend). Interestingly, this project got 16 stars and 7 forks.
    • An Android app which implements the idea proposed in my paperVisual speaker authentication with random prompt texts by a dual-task CNN framework. We perform authentication with liveness detection only with visual information, specifically, the lip region. The code has not been open-sourced due to business limits. click here for video demo
